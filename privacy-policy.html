Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Effective for all Yunok Tech apps

🔒 Our Privacy Commitment

Yunok Tech is deeply committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect, store, upload or share your personal data on any external server. All app data remains exclusively on your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Yunok Tech ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how our Android applications — NotiVault (Notification History & Recovery) and Hanuman Chalisa — handle your information.

By installing and using our apps, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. If you have questions, contact us at yunoktech@gmail.com.

2. Apps Covered by This Policy

  • NotiVault — Notification History & Recovery
    Package: com.yunoktech.notificationhistory
  • Hanuman Chalisa — Offline Devotional App
    Package: com.yunok.hanuman.app

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

Yunok Tech apps are built with a strict privacy-first approach. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Notification content or message data from NotiVault
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID)
  • Browsing history or usage patterns uploaded to servers
  • Financial or payment information
  • Contacts, call logs, or SMS data

4. NotiVault — Data Handling

NotiVault uses the Android Notification Listener Service to capture and store notifications. Here is how this data is handled:

  • Local Storage Only: All notification data is stored exclusively in your device's local SQLite database. It is never uploaded to any server.
  • No Cloud Sync: There is no cloud backup, sync, or account system. Your notification history lives only on your device.
  • No Third-Party Access: Notification data is never shared with advertisers, analytics providers, or any third parties.
  • User Control: You can delete all stored notification data at any time from within the app settings.
  • Auto-Clean: You can configure automatic deletion of old notifications (7, 15, 30 or 60 days).

The Notification Listener permission is used solely to capture and store notifications on your device for your personal use. This permission is never used to monitor, analyze, or transmit your data.

5. Hanuman Chalisa — Data Handling

The Hanuman Chalisa app is a fully offline devotional application. It does not require any permissions that access personal data. Specifically:

  • 100% Offline: All devotional content (Chalisa, Bajrang Baan, Aarti, Mantras) is bundled within the app. No internet connection is needed or used for content.
  • No Personal Data: The app does not request access to contacts, location, camera, microphone or any personal data.
  • Local Preferences Only: User preferences such as language settings and Jap Mala counter values are stored locally using SharedPreferences on your device.

6. Permissions Used

NotiVault uses the following permissions:

  • BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE: Required to capture incoming notifications and store them locally on your device.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Allows the notification listener to restart automatically when your device reboots.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+): To show a persistent status bar indicator that the service is running.

Hanuman Chalisa uses the following permissions:

  • No sensitive permissions. The app may request internet access for optional, non-intrusive advertising only.

7. Advertising

Our apps may display advertisements powered by Google AdMob to support free app development. Google AdMob may use device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy to serve relevant ads. You may opt out of personalized ads through your device's Google account settings.

We do not control the data practices of advertising partners. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

8. Third-Party Services

Our apps may integrate the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For in-app advertising. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates. Subject to Google's Terms of Service.

We do not use analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools or user tracking libraries that transmit personal data.

9. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately at yunoktech@gmail.com.

10. Data Security

Since all user-generated data is stored locally on your device, data security depends on the security of your Android device itself. We strongly recommend:

  • Using a screen lock (PIN, pattern, fingerprint or face unlock)
  • Keeping your Android OS updated to the latest security patch
  • Using Android's built-in encryption for device storage

11. Your Rights

As a user of Yunok Tech apps, you have the following rights:

  • Access: View all data stored by the app (available within the app UI)
  • Deletion: Delete all app data at any time from Settings → App Info → Clear Data
  • Revocation: Revoke any permission granted to the app at any time from Android Settings → Apps → Permissions
  • Uninstallation: Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data
